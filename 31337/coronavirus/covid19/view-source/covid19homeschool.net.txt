				<p><b>Finding Resources During the COVID-19 Pandemic</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are you one of many families who have suddenly found themselves at home&hellip; without having the time to feel properly prepared to jump into having school at home? This list may help equip you as you prepare to keep your child home during unpredictable school closings. These resources are taken from many recommendations of experienced homeschool families.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>THSC has compiled this list to help parents when searching for curriculum and teaching aids. THSC does not endorse or recommend one curriculum over another. This is not an exhaustive list; there are hundreds of amazing curriculum providers with all styles of curriculum available.</p>
<p><b>Resources &ndash; apps &amp; articles to support you in this transition:&nbsp;</b></p>
<ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://thsc.org/why-i-homeschool/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Why I homeschool &ndash; Six families share their top reasons</span></a></li>
