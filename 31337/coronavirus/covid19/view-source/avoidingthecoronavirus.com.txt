<p><strong>Hello everyone!</strong> my name is Roi menashe and I&#8217;m in charge of secuirity at Hadasa mount scoupus hospital in Israel. I decided to create this website in order to share all the information I receive from doctors I meet on daily basis, about how to avoid the coronavirus. menashe</p>



<p>There are a several theories about how the plague broke out, but what we know for sure is that it started in Chaina. probably at Wuhan.</p>



<p>Not all is known about the CoronaVirus. Is is still being studied. nevertheless, we do know how it transfers and the following tips can help you avoid the coronavirus.</p>



<p>First and most important &#8211; the virus spreads and infecting other people by contact. this means it could be transferd by sneezing anc caoughing or by touch. There are a few things you can do to decrase the chances of getting sick.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-large is-resized is-style-default"><img src="https://avoidingthecoronavirus.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/woman-wearing-face-mask-3873197-1-1024x683.jpg" alt="woman wearing mask to prevent coronavirus" class="wp-image-23" width="467" height="311" srcset="https://avoidingthecoronavirus.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/woman-wearing-face-mask-3873197-1-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://avoidingthecoronavirus.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/woman-wearing-face-mask-3873197-1-300x200.jpg 300w, https://avoidingthecoronavirus.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/woman-wearing-face-mask-3873197-1-768x512.jpg 768w, https://avoidingthecoronavirus.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/woman-wearing-face-mask-3873197-1-1536x1024.jpg 1536w" sizes="(max-width: 467px) 100vw, 467px" /></figure>
